    History
  • march 2002 : medias-cité partially finances Localis development, with the Regional Council of Aquitaine, within the framework of its usage for the representation of the public access points in Aquitaine.
  • may 2002 : Localis v0.1 runs on Makina's server.
  • july 2002 : Localis v0.2 is delivered on Médias-cité's server.
  • august 2002 : work on the version 0.3 to make it a more generic tool, usable in other contexts, and GPL release.
  • august 2002 : preparation of another Localis branch at Mer d'Antioche in an interactive version.
  • october 2002 : GPL release of Localis and EasyMapServer
  • december 2002 : GPL release of Localis v0.4.1 ; many features were added with seapass work.
